About Ajeesh

Architecture is storytelling in space

Every wall, window, and threshold tells a story. I help you write yours.

Based in Rajajinagar, Bangalore, I founded Arrow Architect with one belief: great architecture transforms how people live and feel. With a background spanning residential, commercial, and interior design, I bring both technical precision and creative vision to every project — from a compact apartment redesign to a full commercial build.
